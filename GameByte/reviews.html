Video Game Reviews

Tekken 8

Tekken 8 has been highly praised for enhancing the iconic fighting series with fresh elements and modern touches while maintaining its cherished core mechanics. The game introduces a new 'Heat' system, which is a less restrictive version of the Rage Arts from Tekken 7, allowing players to use Heat Smash attacks at any point during a fight, adding strategic depth and flair to battles​

Diablo IV

Diablo 4 has been praised for its dark and immersive narrative, which is considered the strongest in the series so far, with a mix of familiar faces and new characters that enhance its rich lore. The game offers expansive environments ranging from the Druidic haven of Scosglen to the eerie ship graveyard of Hawezar, which players can explore in detail using the new photo mode feature. Gameplay-wise, Diablo 4 introduces Shadow Imbuement and other dynamic combat elements that allow for strategic and visceral battles, making it a robust and engaging experience for both newcomers and long-time fans​

Grand Theft Auto

"Grand Theft Auto V" (GTA 5) offers a vast, detailed open-world experience in the fictional state of San Andreas, primarily centered around the city of Los Santos. The game innovates by providing three distinct playable characters—Michael, Franklin, and Trevor—each with unique backstories and interconnected missions. The narrative explores themes of crime, family, and survival through various engaging missions, including high-stakes heists. The gameplay mechanics are polished, and the world is rich with activities, making it a profound staple in open-world gaming​
